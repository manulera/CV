Summary

During my PhD I used fission yeast to study the mitotic spindle, combining experimental and computational approaches. I grew increasingly interested in FAIR data in the context of model organism genetics, and decided to join PomBase, the model organism database for fission yeast, where I developed quality control pipelines and worked as a biocurator. Now I am working on Genestorian, an Open Source web application that will allow researchers to organise collections of model organism strains and recombinant DNA.
